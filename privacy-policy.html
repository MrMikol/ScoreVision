ScoreVision

Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 2026

ScoreVision does not collect any personally identifiable information. Your privacy is fully respected.

1. Overview

ScoreVision ("the App") is a music sight reading trainer developed by Michael T. This privacy policy explains how the App handles data when you use it.

2. Data We Collect

The App collects only the following anonymous usage data:

  • A randomly generated anonymous ID (not linked to your identity)
  • Number of practice sessions
  • Total time spent in the App
  • Most played game mode and difficulty level

This data contains no personally identifiable information. It cannot be used to identify you in any way.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your location
  • Your device information
  • Any payment information
  • Any other personal information

4. How Data Is Used

The anonymous usage data is used solely to understand how the App is being used overall — for example, how many sessions are being played per week. This helps improve the App experience for all users.

Data is stored locally on your device and periodically summarized and sent to a private spreadsheet accessible only to the developer.

5. Data Storage

Usage data is stored locally on your device for up to one month, after which it is automatically deleted. Weekly summaries are stored securely in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the developer.

6. Third Party Services

The App uses the following third party services:

  • Google Sheets — for receiving anonymous weekly usage summaries
  • Expo / React Native — the framework used to build the App

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

7. Notifications

The App may send local push notifications to remind you to practice. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers. You can disable notifications at any time in the App's Settings or in your phone's notification settings.

8. Children's Privacy

ScoreVision is suitable for users of all ages. The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since no personal information is collected from any user, the App complies with applicable children's privacy laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
